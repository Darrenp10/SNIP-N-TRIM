COMPREHENSIVE SERVICES

From gardening to rubble removal, our winning combination of innovative thinking, scientific design, sophisticated development and ongoing analysis ensure we're delivering to help you grow.



Gardening


Gardening is our priority. Our pride and joy. Anything from moving plants from pot to soil, to just having your garden cleaned and the grass cut, we are here to help. Our team is capable and happy to do the work that you can brag about.


Landscaping


Ever wanted to have that millionaire garden entrance experience? Well, you've come to the right company to help you live that dream. We'll cater for your design exactly how you've dreamed it or we'll put you on the right path, design and build your stunning new landscape from the ground up.


Irrigation installation


Have you ever wanted a laid back, self sufficient gardening eco-system? Here at Snip 'n Trim, we can design and build a fully functioning water irrigation system that will feed your beautiful garden while you sit back, enjoy your coffee or tea, read your book and bask in the sun on a beautiful Saturday morning.


Rubble removal


Whether it's the simple chore of driving to the dump to get rid of that dirt or a large scale rubble clean up, we are fully capable of cleaning up that mess that nobody looks forward to cleaning up.
